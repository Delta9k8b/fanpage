Bio

The band Hippo Campus was formed in 2013 in St Paul, Minnesota. They are considered to be a rock band by some folks' standards. Although it would depend on who you ask; some would consider them to be indie, indie-rock, indie-pop, or like mentioned, just rock. They don't fall into one genre, and their music tends to blur a lot of lines. Their musical themes jump from place to place throughout their discogrophy. Although when listening, you will get the impression that they start to really get a sense of direction for their sound with their first full length album "Landmark" in 2017.
